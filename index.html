About Me

Residing in Durham, North Carolina, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, as well as doing all things outdoors! My hobbies include, hiking, camping, shooting and building guns, and learning survival skills. My interests are many, but if you logged into my YouTube, you would find videos on bushcraft, cooking, mathematics, homesteading, farming, pizza reviews, knot tying, and much much more.

I am currently a Senior at Liberty University Online pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computational Mathematics. I hope to apply the knowledge obtained from this degree towards the Data Science field. I have an estimated graduation date of March, 2023 and am interested in the idea of attending a Master’s in Data Science/Analytics program.

In April 2022, I was honorably discharged from the Marine Corps where I served as a Small Arms Repair Technician, or armorer, at MARSOC. In the Armory, my primary roles dealt with battallion level oversight of the maintenance performed by our armorers. A pretty common day in the armory would be spent analyzing preventative maintenance reports, collaborating with company armorers to ensure proper capture of inspections data, and reading the daily maintenance production report to see the current statuses of items inducted for repair. I am excited about the next chapter of my life, and I hope it goes by much slower than my 4 years in the Marines.

I plan to soon enter the IT field as a Data Analyst, putting together my skills of Python, SQL, and Tableau together to better understand problems that stakeholders may have. I am fueled by my curiosity for finding the optimal solution to problems. I desire to find the best solution for the stakeholder by uncovering the why’s and how’s of their data.
