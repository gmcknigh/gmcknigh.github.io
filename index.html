About

My name is Griffin McKnight and I am a Senior Computational Mathematics student at Liberty University. I enjoy studying mathematics because it allows us to quantitatively describe every level of our universe and daily lives. With this perspective, I enjoy uncovering the why’s and how’s of data through analysis, visualization, and machine learning. There is nothing that excites me more than finding a story behind a dataset and during the process, learning something along the way.

When I’m not studying or working on projects, I enjoy spending my time hiking, learning about plants, and watching YouTube videos about bushcraft, survival, math theory, and coding. Take a look at my resume and projects, connect with me on socials, and reach out if I can help you better use and understand the data in your life.
